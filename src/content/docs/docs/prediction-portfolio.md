title: Prediction Portfolio
description: Track your prediction market positions and performance
---

The prediction portfolio gives you a consolidated view of all your prediction market activity.

## Accessing Your Portfolio

Navigate to **Predictions > Prediction Portfolio** from the navigation menu.

## What's Displayed

### Positions

View all prediction market assets where you hold a position. This includes:

- **Asset symbol** — The prediction market identifier
- **Current balance** — Your holdings in this prediction
- **Average price** — The average price you paid
- **Current value** — Current market value of your position
- **Profit/Loss** — Unrealized gains or losses

### Recent Activity

A feed of your recent prediction market transactions:

- Purchases and sales
- Order placements and cancellations
- Resolution settlements

### Open Orders

Quick access to any open orders you have on prediction market assets, with the ability to cancel them directly from the portfolio view.

## Prediction Margin

For more advanced margin management, visit **Predictions > Prediction Margin**. This view lets you:

- View margin positions on prediction assets
- Adjust collateral levels
- Monitor margin call thresholds

## Tracking Performance

The portfolio tracks your performance across all prediction markets, including:

- Total invested across all predictions
- Current total value
- Overall profit/loss
- Win rate on resolved predictions
