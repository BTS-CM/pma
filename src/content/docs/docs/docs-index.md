The Bitshares Prediction Market UI is a feature-rich desktop application for creating, trading, and managing prediction markets on the Bitshares blockchain. It runs as an Electron app with a web-based interface powered by Astro and React.

## Key Features

- **Zero authentication** — Construct and sign transactions locally without exposing your private keys
- **Multiple broadcast methods** — Sign via QR codes, deep links, or local JSON files
- **Multi-chain support** — Seamlessly switch between Bitshares mainnet and testnet
- **Multi-wallet support** — Compatible with Beet and BeetEOS wallets
- **11 languages** — Fully localized interface
- **Built-in moderation** — Global and personal blocklists protect you from bad actors

## What You Can Do

### Prediction Markets
Browse active and expired prediction markets, create your own with the multi-step wizard, join prediction market organizations (PMOs), and manage your portfolio of positions.

### Trading
Use the built-in decentralized exchange (DEX) for limit and market orders, execute instant one-click trades, or swap tokens through liquidity pools.

### Account Management
Transfer assets, manage your portfolio balances, track open orders, and create new Bitshares accounts.

- A desktop computer running Windows, macOS, or Linux
