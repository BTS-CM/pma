title: Managing Your Portfolio
description: View balances, open orders, and favourites
---

The portfolio section provides a comprehensive view of your Bitshares account holdings and activity.

## Balances

Navigate to **Account > Portfolio Balances** to view all assets you hold.

### What's Shown

- **Asset symbol** — The asset identifier
- **Balance** — Your current holdings
- **Asset issuer** — Who created the asset
- **Actions** — Quick links to trade or transfer

### Managing Favourites

From the balances view, you can add or remove assets from your favourites list. Favourited assets are easily accessible from the Favourites page.

## Open Orders

Navigate to **Account > Portfolio Open Orders** to manage your pending orders.

### Viewing Orders

See all your open limit orders across all markets in one place:

- **Market pair** — The two assets being traded
- **Side** — Buy or sell
- **Price** — Your limit price
- **Amount** — Remaining amount to be filled
- **Created** — When the order was placed

### Cancelling Orders

Cancel individual orders directly from this view. The cancellation generates a deep link for your wallet to sign the `limit_order_cancel` operation.

## Favourites

Navigate to **Account > Favourites** to manage your saved items.

### Favourite Assets

Quick access to assets you trade frequently. From the favourites view you can:

- View current market data
- Jump directly to the DEX for that asset
- Remove from favourites

### Favourite Users

Save accounts you interact with regularly. From the favourites view you can:

- View account details
- Quick transfer to a favourited user
- Remove from favourites

Both favourite lists use virtualized rendering for smooth performance even with many entries.
